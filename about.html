About Us

Yassh Pharma supply quality nutraceutical products to cater the needs of various therapeutic segments like women's health, bone health, liver health, digestive health, nerve & cognitive health, Immunity boosters, skin & hair care etc.

We value the customers satisfaction by providing the range of suitable products to the customers. Our products undergo lab testing locally to ensure safety.

We value the customer relationship across the spectrum of healthcare Industry. We are specialised and experienced in providing suitable alternatives with phytopharmaceuticals formulations for day-to-day use.

Yassh Pharma outsource unique and innovative products for the community for better outcomes. We provide quality services and strive hard for customer satisfaction through our products and services.
